"Essence of tubule top 50 by uniqueness

This is slightly different from some of the other top 50s. Here, I've ranked genes according to their signal in that tissue divided by the signal in all other tissues. It seems to emphasise uniqueness quite well.

This table shows uniqueness of larval and adult tubule combined, compared with all other tissues. It thus provides a view on the "essence of tubulehood". This appears to be hardcore transport (organic cation, organic anion, visual pigments, multivitamins, phosphate, fatty acids, Na+/iodide); urate oxidase, and alakline phosphatase.
